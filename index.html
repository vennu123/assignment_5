something about mangas


Manga are comics or graphic novels origination from Japan. The term manga is used in Japanto refer to both comics and cartooning. The medium includes works in a broad range of geners : action,comedy,detective,drama,historical,horror,mystery, romance,science fiction,fantasy,sports and games. Manga stories are printed in black and white due to time constraints,artistic reasons and to keep the printing costs low. A manga artist (also known as mangaka) works with a few assistants in a small studio and is associated with a creative editor from a commercial publishing company. Mangas are usually serialized in large manga magazines. If a manga is popular enough it may be animated after or during its run.

some intersesting facts about manga:


  • most of the manga characters have big eyes. This universal trend was started by Oasmu Tezuka, creator of legendary Astroboy
  • Japanese is always read from right to left. so the same system applies to manga comic strips
  • several "manga cafes " are available in Japan. Readers can enjoy their favorite mangas with a cup of coffee
  • most characters in manga comics have a dark side. Some hide it very well while others dont
  • manga is probably originated back to the 12th century
  • a manga can have a mix of black & white pages and some with colors
